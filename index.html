Introduction

YUNE brand interior describes what we stand for as a team and which values ​​are of enormous importance within our team.

Why

We believe that behaviour changes the boundaries of creative production.

Behavior is more changeable than ever due to modern media. We click, swipe and scroll around a bit. Our subconscious brain curates dozens of articles, photos, videos and social media posts every day. We have taught ourselves to filter very quickly and see which content we do and do not want to consume. In order to know which brand content appeals to the target group, we at YUNE find it crucial to recognize, analyse, analyze and apply current behavior within our work.

How

By using scientific studies, data and proofpoints, our content taps into subconscious human behaviour.

As an agency, we dig through where others stop. We have various in-house and partner solutions available for each phase of a project or campaign with which we help the customer and ultimately the target group. From tailor-made 24-hour market research to an EEG scan to measure the impact of our videos, at YUNE we do everything we can to understand the subconscious brain and make brands more human.

What

We produce impactful digital content: Content - Video - Social

This is where we excel and can provide the most added value for our customers. It helps us to keep focus in our daily work.
